Purpose

  • Mission Statment
  • Benefits

Our goal is to offer free online tutoring for students in grades K-8 during the outbreak, due to limited teacher-to-student interaction. We want to use our experience with kids and high-level education to provide students with the same interactive learning they would receive if we were still going to school at this time.

  • Distance is NOT an obstacle (accessible to students outside of the DUSD)
  • YOU choose when you want to be tutored, how often, and for how long
  • Sessions can occur over preferred method of interaction, including email or video-call, or any other requests you may have (we’re flexible!)
  • Individualized method of teaching for every student based on their needs
  • Handful of qualified high school juniors that can teach English, Math, Science, World Languages, and more!
